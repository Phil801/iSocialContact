iSocialContact gives you a beautiful and familiar interface to present your Social and Contact info on.

== Description ==
iSocialContact addresses the cluttering of your site with dozens of social and contact icons and links.

It comes with over 20 icons for the most popular social networks and contact methods.  
The layout is completely customizable and you can add new icons as well.

Once you install the plugin, just add its widget to a sidebar.

You can select which icon you want to show up in each slot on the phone, customize the link to point to your personal account and customize the tooltip that pops up when you hover over it with your mouse.

== Installation ==

Upload the iSocialContact plugin to your blog, Activate it, add its widget to your sidebar and customize the settings!
